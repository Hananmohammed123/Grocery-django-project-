About Online Grocery Shop

We are Creative Tech Enthusiasts working since 2015

Grocery Shop (Innovative Retail Concepts Private Limited) is India's largest online food and grocery store. With a catalogue boasting over 18,000 products and more than 1,000 brands, you can find everything you need, including fresh fruits and vegetables, rice and dals, spices and seasonings, packaged products, beverages, personal care items, and meats.

Grocery Shop ensures a wide range of options in each category, carefully selected to offer the best quality at the lowest prices. You can choose a convenient delivery time slot, and your order will be delivered right to your doorstep in cities like Bangalore, Hyderabad, Mumbai, Pune, Chennai, Delhi, Noida, Mysore, Coimbatore, Vijayawada-Guntur, Kolkata, Ahmedabad-Gandhinagar, Lucknow-Kanpur, Gurgaon, Vadodara, Visakhapatnam, Surat, Nagpur, Patna, Indore, and Chandigarh Tricity. Payment options include online payments with debit/credit cards or cash/Sodexo on delivery.

Grocery Shop guarantees on-time delivery and top-notch quality. Happy Shopping!
